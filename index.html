About

ABOUT ME

My name is Mike, I'm a recent graduate of Software Engineering and looking for work in the field of Game Development. I've been a gamer since I was 3 starting with my first game, Kirby's Dreamland on the original Game Boy. I have been passionate about video games all my life, while other kids wanted to be astronauts I knew I wanted to make video games. At age 11 I spent free time in classes doodling in my notebooks plans for games, maps, drawing characters & making lists of potential mission ideas. Where others went to sports camp I went to several tech related camps including one for game dev where I got my first (though very basic) introduction. into programming. In High school I took computer programming and computer engineering and now with graduating college I'm ready to make my dream a reality!
